# How to Add Your Profile Photo

## Steps:

1. **Save your profile picture** (the one with you in the maroon shirt and sunglasses) to your computer

2. **Rename it to:** `astitva-profile.jpg`

3. **Copy it to the public folder:**
   - Navigate to: `/Users/astitvajha/Downloads/website/study-link-world-main/public/`
   - Paste the `astitva-profile.jpg` file there

4. **Refresh your browser** - Your photo will now appear in:
   - The "About the Founder" section (large avatar)
   - All 4 positions in the "Meet Our Team" section

## Alternative Method (Drag & Drop):

1. Open Finder and navigate to the `public` folder in your project
2. Drag and drop your profile picture into the `public` folder
3. Rename it to `astitva-profile.jpg`
4. Refresh your browser
